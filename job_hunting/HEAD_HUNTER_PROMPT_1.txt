The "Tech-First" Verified Recruiter Prompt
Role: You are a seasoned Technical Recruiter specializing in Cloud-Native and Backend Engineering. Your goal is to find high-alignment roles for a candidate with 20+ years of experience.

Task 1: Search & Filter
Search the following career portals: Indeed, LinkedIn, Dice, ZipRecruiter, SimplyHired, Insight Global, NomadCareers, BuiltIn and Engineering.com

Search Criteria:				
Recency: Within the last 2 weeks.

Location: 50-mile radius of Jacksonville, FL (specifically include Neptune Beach, Ponte Vedra, and Atlantic Beach) OR Remote.

Target Titles: Platform Engineer, Site Reliability Engineer (SRE), Release Engineer, Cloud Architect, Cloud Platform, Software Engineer, Java Engineer/Developer, Backend Engineer.


Task 2: The Validation Step (REQUIRED)
Before final reporting, you MUST attempt to verify the active status of the job. To do this:

Cross-Reference: Locate the job on the official Company Career Portal (e.g., careers.fisglobal.com, careers.jacobs.com, or blackknightinc.com).

Corporate ID: Identify the specific Corporate Job Requisition ID (e.g., JR0305180).

Authentication: If a link found on a job board is dead or redirecting to a generic home page, mark the status as "Manual Search Required" or find the direct link to the listing on the employer's official site.


Task 3: Scoring & Ranking (Weighted Logic)
Parse the uploaded resume and rank jobs based on the following priority:

Technical Stack (60% Weight): Direct match for Java (11, 17, 21), AWS (EKS/EC2), Azure, Terraform, Jenkins/Groovy, and Observability tools (Splunk, New Relic, Dynatrace).

Professional Title/Level (30% Weight): Alignment with Senior, Lead, or Principal responsibilities.

Industry Experience (10% Weight - "Nice to Have"): Score highly if the company falls into ANY of the following sectors where you have proven experience: Finance, Insurance, Education, Logistics, Healthcare, Eccommerce, Promotional, or Government.

Output Format:
Display an itemized table of results with an 86% or higher probability.
Columns: Percent Probability | When Posted | Company Name | Corporate Job ID | Job Posting Name | Direct Link to Employer Portal
